About Founder

Hi, I’m Suniel Guptaa

I’m a Mentoria-certified career counsellor, ICF-certified life coach, and soft skills trainer. But before any of that, I spent more than three decades working in the corporate world. I’ve worked in business development, sales management, and training leadership, including leading the training department of a major pharmaceutical company. Over the years, one thing became very clear to me: People with opportunities don’t struggle because they lack talent or will power. They struggle because they lack clarity, confidence, and guidance. That’s why I now dedicate my work to helping people grow—whether that means choosing the right career, becoming a better leader, or simply finding direction in life. I’ve had the privilege of training 10,000+ professionals and students across industries. I’m also the author of What Next? Journey to a Successful Career, a practical guide for students and job seekers navigating today’s professional world. My programmes cover: • Behavioural Training: Shifting mindsets for long-term success. • Skills-Based Training: Practical tools for communication, leadership, and efficiency. • Personalised Coaching: Goal-oriented sessions designed to unlock your unique potential.

How I Can Help

Everyone’s journey is different, but from students and corporate professionals to business owners and C-suite executives, people come to me for one of these reasons:

Communication Skills

Effective communication is a muscle that requires exercise.

Being a ‘smart communicator’ means expressing ideas clearly, handling difficult conversations, or building strong professional relationships.

Coach Suniel’s Communication Skills Training is designed to help you and your team:

Establish Trust: Build stronger connections through active listening and observation.

Boost Productivity: Use proven strategies to minimise workplace friction.

Gather Intelligence: Master the art of questioning to uncover key information.

Whether you are a student stepping into the world, a job seeker striving for that breakthrough, or a professional levelling up, this programme is designed for your specific goals.

  • Best for: Students, job seekers, and professionals
  • Duration: 12–15 hours
  • Format: Online or offline
  • Personalisation: Fully customised curriculum for individuals or groups

Management Skills

Managing people is very different from doing the work yourself.

Many professionals are promoted into management roles without ever being taught how to lead a team. This workshop helps new and experienced managers learn how to guide people, run effective meetings, and achieve results through their teams.

Great management isn’t just about oversight; it’s about inspiration. Our Management Training Programme equips you with the emotional intelligence and strategic vision to meet organisational objectives.

  • Duration: 8-hour intensive workshop
  • Format: Optimised for offline, interactive group settings
  • Group size: 6–15 participants
  • Experience Level: First-time managers and experienced leaders

Leadership Skills

Leadership isn’t about a title—it’s about influence.

To be a compelling leader, you must be confident in your own skin.

Our Leadership Excellence Programme is a deep dive into the attitudes and behaviours that define a capable leader. You will learn to retain, engage, and empower your people to see extraordinary results.

  • Duration: Two-day intensive
  • Format: Group setting (8–16 participants)
  • Ideal for: Corporate leaders and professionals currently in leadership roles

Life Coaching

A Life Coach doesn’t give you a map; they help you find your own compass.

You may feel stuck, uncertain, or pulled in too many directions.

I create a space for you to step back, reflect, and move forward with clarity. Instead of giving advice, I help you explore your own answers—and build the confidence to act on them.

Since 2015, I’ve worked with 250+ individuals across career, personal growth, and life transitions, helping my clients move from ‘stuck’ to ‘successful.’

Job Interview Coaching

Transformation isn’t a sprint; it’s a series of intentional strides.

The transition from classroom to boardroom is often the toughest challenge a professional will face. Sometimes even the smartest candidates miss opportunities simply because they don’t know how to position themselves in interviews.

To win in a hyper-competitive market, you must bridge the gap between being capable and being desirable. My Career Readiness & Employability Programme is designed to make you the candidate employers are looking for.

The flexible 8-hour intensive programme is designed for individual excellence.

How it works:

  • Total Duration: 8 hours of 1-on-1 or small group coaching.
  • The Schedule: Broken into 4 focused sessions (2 hours each) to allow for reflection and real-world application between meetings.
  • Your Setting: Available online (via Zoom/Google Meet) or offline (in person) based on your preference and location.
  • Tailored Content: Every hour is customised to your specific career goals.

Why the ‘4-Session’ Split?

I’ve found that the 2-hour session format is the sweet spot for professional growth. It’s long enough to dive deep into complex behavioural shifts, but short enough to keep your energy and focus at their peak.
